Representation and content. Who gets to drive this story? Note the diversity of the speaking cast as evidenced by the text (names, stated identities, roles) without inventing demographics the script doesn't state. Are characters from underrepresented groups written with agency and interiority, or as props/stereotypes? Separately, note content that will drive rating and audience decisions: violence, language, sexual content, substance use, and how integral each is to the story. Score on the quality and intentionality of representation and content choices — not on hitting a quota.
