Production complexity — scored from a producer's chair, where a LOWER burden is a BETTER score. Count what the digest shows: number of distinct locations, cast size, night exteriors, water/weather work, VFX and creature work, stunts, period setting, crowd scenes, children or animals. "Excellent" means contained and shootable (few locations, small cast, minimal effects); "weak" means the page demands far more than any plausible budget tier for this material.
